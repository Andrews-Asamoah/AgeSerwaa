About Us

Akwaaba!

We started operating on 19th May 2015

Named after Agnes Osei and Ama Serwaa all of Offinso Municipal. Ageserwaa Guest House is a stylish Guest House at the core heart of Offinso Municipal. Offering 23 rooms to its clients, the Guest house is small enough to give a special attention to every guest. This Guest house features a restaurant and inside bar.
Each room is ensuite with TVs, Fridge and Large wardrobe with comfortable beds. There are four different room types so there is bound to be the right room for you, whether honeymoon suite, chember and hall to accomodate a family. There are security Pernonels who keep watch 24/7
